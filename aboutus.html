About Us

LEEMCo is an evolving start-up developed by Wayne State University (Detroit, MI, USA) researchers driven by the vision to enable environmentally sensitive electricity (ESE). 

The primary asset of LEEMCo is the Locational Emissions Estimation Methodology (LEEM) which was developed to provide location-specific, real-time and forecasted marginal emissions information.  LEEM employs a sophisticated system of databases, algorithms, and power flow models that tracks, organizes, normalizes, analyzes, and reports out a myriad of real-time and forecasted electricity cost and emissions information that can be used by companies, utilities, “big energy data” firms, energy management firms, governments, individuals and others to make better, and more informed, energy and emission management decisions.

Three LEEM-enabled products are currently available:  HERO, PEPSO, and SmartCharge.  HERO (Home Emissions Read-Out) is an android app that provides up-to-the minute marginal emissions information at the click of a button; PEPSO (Polluting Emissions Pump Station Optimization) is a software package that performs optimization of large water distribution systems including both cost and environmental (emissions) objectives; and SmartCharge is a tool for rechargeable house-hold devices that operates in response to the marginal emissions information provided to the charger through a wireless connection.   

The LEEMCo business model includes the sale of LEEM-enabled products and software, as well as the direct sale/licensing of the marginal emissions data stream for user-designed applications.
